Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026 · Australian Privacy Principles (Privacy Act 1988 (Cth))

1. Who we are

Sageroom ("we", "us", "our") is operated from Queensland, Australia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

By using Sageroom, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

Account information: Your email address when you register.

Conversation content: The questions and prompts you submit, and the AI-generated responses. This content is stored so you can access your conversation history.

Usage data: Token counts and cost per API call, used to enforce fair-use limits and manage AI costs. Session metadata (timestamps, brain selected) used for monitoring and billing.

Billing data: We do not store your payment card details. Stripe processes payments and stores card information under their own privacy policy.

Technical data: IP address and request metadata logged by our infrastructure for security and diagnostics. We never log the content of your messages in application logs.

3. How we use your information

  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To deliver AI responses to your questions
  • To store and display your conversation history
  • To enforce per-user AI cost limits ($8.00 AUD/month)
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To send you service-related emails (account confirmation, billing receipts, material changes to these policies)
  • To monitor platform health and security

We do not use your conversation content to train AI models, for advertising, or for any purpose beyond delivering the service.

4. Third-party processors

We share data with the following parties solely to operate the service. Each is bound by their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic — Your messages are transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate AI responses. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy. Anthropic does not train models on API inputs by default.
  • Supabase — Stores your account data, conversation history, and usage records. Data may be stored in the US or Australia depending on your Supabase region. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — Processes subscription payments. Your email is shared with Stripe for billing purposes. Stripe stores your payment card details. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — Hosts our application servers. Application logs (containing request metadata, never message content) are retained by Railway. See Railway's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. International data transfers

Some of our processors (Anthropic, Stripe, Railway) may store or process your data outside Australia, primarily in the United States. We take reasonable steps to ensure these processors provide adequate data protection. By using Sageroom, you consent to this transfer.

6. Cookies and local storage

Sageroom uses browser local storage to store your authentication session token. This is essential for the service to function — without it, you would be logged out on every page load. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics cookies. No third-party cookies are set by Sageroom.

7. Data retention

Conversation history: Retained indefinitely so you can access your history. Deleted immediately when you delete your account.

Billing records: Retained for 7 years as required by Australian tax law, even after account deletion.

Application logs: Retained for 30 days by Railway, after which they are automatically deleted.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), row-level security on all database tables, and server-side secrets management. However, no system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@sageroom.com.

9. Children

Sageroom is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us at privacy@sageroom.com and we will delete the account promptly.

10. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Delete your account and all associated personal data
  • Complain about how we handle your personal information

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@sageroom.com. You may also delete your account directly from the account settings page, which immediately triggers deletion of all personal data (except billing records required by law).

We will respond to access and correction requests within 30 days.

11. Notifiable data breaches

In the event of an eligible data breach under the Privacy Act 1988, we will notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) and all affected individuals within 30 days of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches (NDB) scheme.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by email of material changes at least 14 days before they take effect. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated.

13. Contact and complaints

Privacy enquiries: privacy@sageroom.com

If you are not satisfied with our response to a privacy complaint, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC):

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026 · Australian Privacy Principles (Privacy Act 1988)

1. Who we are

Sageroom is operated from Australia. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

2. What we collect

We collect your email address when you create an account. We store your conversation history and group session content so you can access it later. We record AI usage data (token counts and cost) to enforce fair-use limits and manage costs. We do not collect your name, phone number, or payment card details directly — card processing is handled by Stripe.

3. How we use your information

We use your email to authenticate your account and send you service-related notifications. Conversation content is used solely to provide the service — it is not used to train AI models. Usage data is used to enforce per-user cost limits and generate internal reports.

4. Third-party processors

We share data with the following third parties to operate the service:

  • Anthropic — AI responses are generated via Anthropic's API. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's servers to produce responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — We use Supabase to store your account data and conversation history. Data is stored in Australia or the US depending on your selected Supabase region.
  • Stripe — Subscription billing is handled by Stripe. We share your email with Stripe for billing purposes. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — Our application servers run on Railway. Application logs may contain metadata (not message content) about your requests.

5. Data retention

Your conversations are stored indefinitely so you can access your history. You may delete your account at any time, which permanently removes all your data within 30 days. We retain billing records for 7 years as required by Australian tax law.

6. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, email privacy@sageroom.com. You may also delete your account directly from the account page, which triggers deletion of all personal data.

7. Notifiable data breaches

In the event of an eligible data breach under the Privacy Act 1988, we will notify the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) and affected individuals within 30 days of becoming aware of the breach.

8. Contact

Privacy questions or complaints: privacy@sageroom.com. If we do not resolve your complaint satisfactorily, you may contact the OAIC at oaic.gov.au.
